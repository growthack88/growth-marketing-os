# Bilingual Hook Bank (EN + AR)

> 30 hook formulas tested with real ad spend and organic reach across MENA + global audiences. Each hook: English version, Egyptian Arabic version, and the trigger behind it.

## ⚡ What it does

Hooks are the highest-leverage 5 seconds in marketing. This bank gives you plug-and-play openers organized by psychological trigger — and unlike every English swipe file on the internet, each one has a native Arabic version that actually sounds human, not translated.

## 🎯 When I use it (real scenario)

Built from hooks that survived paid campaigns for e-com and info products across Egypt and GCC. Rule of thumb from testing: the same offer with a stronger hook has repeatedly cut CPC 20-40% before touching anything else.

## 📋 The Bank (sample — 10 of 30)

**CONTRARIAN (breaks a belief)**
1. EN: "Your ads aren't the problem. Your offer is."
   AR: "مشكلتك مش في الإعلانات. مشكلتك في الـ Offer نفسه."
2. EN: "Stop posting daily. Seriously."
   AR: "بطّل تنشر كل يوم. بجد."

**LOSS / MISTAKE (fear of waste)**
3. EN: "I burned $10K on Meta ads so you don't have to."
   AR: "حرقت 10 آلاف دولار في إعلانات ميتا عشان انت متعملش كده."
4. EN: "The pricing mistake that kills 80% of new stores."
   AR: "غلطة التسعير اللي بتقفل 8 من كل 10 متاجر جديدة."

**SPECIFIC RESULT (proof-first)**
5. EN: "From 1.7 to 2.8 ROAS in 7 days — without new creatives."
   AR: "من 1.7 لـ 2.8 ROAS في أسبوع — من غير ولا creative جديد."

**CURIOSITY GAP**
6. EN: "There's a metric in your dashboard nobody checks. It predicts churn 30 days early."
   AR: "في رقم في الداشبورد بتاعك محدش بيبص عليه — بيتنبأ بالـ churn قبلها بشهر."

**CALL-OUT (audience filter)**
7. EN: "If you run ads under $3K/month, this changes your whole strategy."
   AR: "لو بتصرف أقل من 3 آلاف دولار في الشهر على إعلانات — البوست ده هيغير استراتيجيتك."

**QUESTION TRIGGER**
8. EN: "What does AI say when someone asks about your brand?"
   AR: "الـ AI بيقول عنك ايه لما حد يسأل عن البراند بتاعك؟"

**TIME / EFFORT COLLAPSE**
9. EN: "A 5-minute audit that replaces a $500 consultation."
   AR: "Audit في 5 دقايق بيوفر عليك استشارة بـ 500 دولار."

**STORY OPEN**
10. EN: "A client almost fired his whole ad team last month. The data said otherwise."
    AR: "الشهر اللي فات عميل كان هيمشّي فريق الإعلانات كله. الأرقام قالت حاجة تانية."

*(Full 30 in the repo — this file grows monthly.)*

## 🧠 Pro tips

- Never translate hooks literally — rebuild them in عامية. Formal Arabic (فصحى) kills scroll-stopping power in ads.
- Pair CONTRARIAN hooks with proof in line 2 or you lose credibility.
- Test hooks as text overlays on the same video before producing new creatives.
