# COD WhatsApp Script Bank (EN + AR)

> 16 WhatsApp scripts covering the stages your store analytics don't show: confirming the order, saving the delivery, and turning a delivered package into the next sale. English + native عامية versions.

## ⚡ What it does

In COD markets the order isn't won at checkout — it's won on WhatsApp: the confirmation message that gets answered, the delivery-day heads-up that gets someone home, the recovery message after a failed attempt. These scripts cover that pipeline stage by stage, written in the register that gets replies — short, human, one question at a time — instead of the call-center template voice that gets left on read.

## 🎯 When I use it (real scenario)

Built from operating my own COD store, where WhatsApp handles what the platform can't see: the gap between "order placed" and "cash collected" (the exact stages in the [COD Profit Funnel](../../frameworks/cod-profit-funnel.md)). The register rule came from watching reply rates: messages that read like a business broadcast get ignored; messages that read like the shop owner personally texting — «معاك أحمد من المتجر» — get answered in minutes. The scripts bake that in.

## 📋 The Bank

**STAGE 1 — ORDER CONFIRMATION (send within the hour)**
1. EN: "Hi [name] 👋 [Your name] from [store]. Got your order for [product] — just confirming before we ship: still good to go? (Total [X] incl. shipping, pay on delivery)"
   AR: «أهلًا يا [الاسم] 👋 معاك [اسمك] من [المتجر]. وصلنا الأوردر بتاع [المنتج] — بس بأكد معاك قبل الشحن: تمام نبعته؟ (الإجمالي [X] شامل الشحن، والدفع عند الاستلام)»
2. Follow-up if no reply (24h) — EN: "Quick reminder about your [product] order — reply YES and it ships today 🚚"
   AR: «تذكير سريع بخصوص أوردر الـ [المنتج] — رد بـ"تمام" وهيتشحن النهارده 🚚»
   *Why: the total-with-shipping in message 1 kills the #1 doorstep surprise. One question, one-word answer.*

**STAGE 2 — ADDRESS & PHONE FIX**
3. EN: "One small thing — the address says [X]. Which building/landmark should the courier ask for?"
   AR: «حاجة صغيرة بس — العنوان مكتوب [X]. الدليفري يسأل عن أنهي عمارة أو علامة مميزة؟»
4. EN: "The courier will call this number [X] — is that the best one on delivery day?"
   AR: «الدليفري هيتصل على الرقم ده [X] — هو ده أحسن رقم يوم التسليم؟»
   *Why: asking for a landmark beats asking to "verify your address" — it's concrete and no one feels accused of lying.*

**STAGE 3 — DELIVERY DAY (the delivery-rate saver)**
5. EN: "Your [product] is out for delivery today between [window] 📦 Will someone be around?"
   AR: «الـ [المنتج] بتاعك خارج للتوصيل النهارده بين [الفترة] 📦 حد هيكون موجود؟»
6. If "not home" — EN: "No problem — tomorrow same time, or should the courier call to arrange?"
   AR: «ولا يهمك — بكرة نفس المعاد، ولا الدليفري يتصل بيك تتفقوا؟»
   *Why: message 5 alone rescues the missed-at-home failure — the #1 cause of RTO.*

**STAGE 4 — FAILED ATTEMPT RECOVERY (same day, not next week)**
7. EN: "The courier couldn't reach you today — totally fine. When works this week? We'll hold your [product]."
   AR: «الدليفري محدرش يوصلك النهارده — عادي خالص. إيه أنسب يوم الأسبوع ده؟ محجوزلك الـ [المنتج]»
8. Second failure — EN: "Want us to try one last time [day], or deliver to your work/another address instead?"
   AR: «تحب نحاول آخر مرة يوم [اليوم]، ولا نوصله على الشغل أو عنوان تاني؟»
   *Why: offering the alternative address converts refusals you'd otherwise write off. After two honest attempts, let it go — blacklist repeat refusers.*

**STAGE 5 — POST-DELIVERY (the cheapest revenue you'll ever make)**
9. Day 1 — EN: "Did the [product] arrive in good shape? Anything off, tell me directly 🙏"
   AR: «الـ [المنتج] وصلك تمام؟ لو في أي حاجة مش مظبوطة قولّي على طول 🙏»
10. Day 7 review ask — EN: "If you're enjoying [product], a quick review here helps us a lot: [link]"
    AR: «لو الـ [المنتج] عجبك، تقييم سريع هنا بيفرق معانا جدًا: [اللينك]»
11. Day 14 upsell — EN: "Customers who got [product] usually pair it with [product 2] — want me to reserve one at [offer]?"
    AR: «اللي بيشتروا [المنتج] غالبًا بياخدوا معاه [المنتج 2] — تحب أحجزلك واحد بـ[العرض]؟»
    *Why: the upsell lands ONLY after 9 and 10 established this number as a human relationship, not a promo channel.*

**GUARDRAIL SCRIPTS**
12. Opt-out respect — EN: "All good — I'll only message you about your orders from now on 👍"
    AR: «تمام — مش هبعتلك غير بخصوص الأوردرات بس من هنا ورايح 👍»
13-16. Objection one-liners for confirmation calls: shipping cost («الشحن [X] لأي حتة في [البلد] — والمعاينة قبل الدفع»), delivery time, exchange policy, "is this real?" («المتجر شغال من [سنة] — شوف تقييمات العملاء هنا: [لينك]»).

## 🧠 Pro tips

- Sender identity beats copy: a real first name + store name outperforms the store name alone — people reply to people.
- One question per message, answerable in one word. Every extra question halves the reply rate.
- Never send stage 5 messages from a different number than stages 1-4 — the relationship IS the number.
- Time-box the pipeline: confirmation within 1 hour, failed-attempt recovery same day. Every hour of delay is delivery-rate decay.
- WhatsApp Business labels ARE your funnel dashboard: label by stage (تأكيد/شحن/اتسلم/رفض) and you can read your confirmation and delivery rates at a glance.
