# Worked Example: The COD Store ROAS Collapse

> 🧪 **This is a WORKED EXAMPLE — a fictional but realistic scenario built to teach the decision process.** "Nour Home" is not a real client. Every number is anchored to a published benchmark (cited inline) so the math is honest even though the store is invented. What's real here is the method.

## ⚡ What it does

Teaches the single most valuable skill in performance marketing — diagnosing before treating — by walking through a complete COD e-commerce turnaround: the panic, the wrong instinct, the funnel decomposition, the fix priority, and the week-by-week decisions. You can run the same walkthrough on your own store using the linked assets at each step.

## 🎯 The Scenario

**"Nour Home"** — a fictional Cairo-based home-goods store. Meta ads, checkout via cart permalinks, ~90% cash on delivery (Egyptian shoppers' stated COD preference runs 55-70%; [Consultancy-me, 2024](https://www.consultancy-me.com/news/11138/the-rise-of-online-shopping-and-ecommerce-in-egypt)).

The Monday message every media buyer knows: *"ROAS was 2.6 three weeks ago. It's 1.4 now. We need new creatives — can we shoot this week?"*

New creatives cost a week and real money. Before spending either, we diagnose.

## 📋 The Walkthrough

### Step 1 — Refuse the first diagnosis (Day 1)

"Creative fatigue" is the most common self-diagnosis in e-commerce and it's wrong more often than it's right. We run the [Meta Ads Creative Diagnosis](../prompts/paid-ads/meta-ads-creative-diagnosis.md) with 14-day windows:

| Layer | Before | Now | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|
| CTR (link) | 2.2% | 2.1% | Fine — right at the e-com median of 2.19% ([Triple Whale, 35K brands, 2025](https://www.triplewhale.com/blog/facebook-ads-benchmarks)) |
| CPM | E£ equiv. of ~$1.85 | ~$1.90 | Fine — Egypt's Meta CPM runs ~$1.81 ([Lebesgue, 2026](https://lebesgue.io/facebook-ads/facebook-cpm-by-country)) |
| Frequency | 2.1 | 2.3 | Not fatigue territory |
| Landing page views / clicks | 78% | 77% | Fine |
| **Checkout started → purchase** | **41%** | **26%** | 🔴 **Collapsed** |

**The lesson:** the ad layer is healthy. People click like before, land like before, add to cart like before — then vanish at checkout. New creatives would have changed nothing except the bank balance. (Shopify checkout-completion average is ~45%; top stores hit 59-66% — [Littledata, 2025](https://www.littledata.io/average/checkout-completion-rate).)

### Step 2 — Find what changed (Day 1, an hour later)

Checkout collapses have boring causes. We ask one question: *"what did anyone change in the last month?"* The answer in this scenario — and in real life, astonishingly often: **shipping fee was raised and moved to appear only at checkout**, to "protect the conversion rate on the product page."

That's backwards, and the data says so: unexpected extra costs are the **#1 cart abandonment reason six years running (48% of abandoners)** — [Baymard Institute, 2025](https://baymard.com/lists/cart-abandonment-rate). Hiding the fee doesn't protect conversion; it moves the shock to the worst possible moment.

### Step 3 — Fix the offer layer first (Week 1)

Ranked by impact ÷ effort (the [CRO Teardown](../prompts/cro/landing-page-cro-teardown.md) ordering):

1. **Show total cost before checkout** — shipping fee on the offer page, under the buy button: «الشحن 60 جنيه لأي حتة في مصر — والدفع عند الاستلام». Cost: one line of copy.
2. **Free-shipping threshold** just above the AOV to convert the fee objection into a bundle nudge.
3. **COD reassurance microcopy** under every buy button (the [Arabic CTA Bank](../swipe-files/ctas/arabic-cta-bank.md), items 5-7).

No redesign. No new creatives. Copy changes shipped in an afternoon.

### Step 4 — The COD blind spot (Week 2)

While inside the account we ask the question the dashboard never answers: of the orders placed, **how many turned into cash at the door?** In COD markets, checkout is a promise, not a payment ([The COD Profit Funnel](../frameworks/cod-profit-funnel.md)). Nour Home tracked nothing after "order placed."

Published proxies say untracked COD operations leak badly: COD orders fail at **25-30%** vs 2-8% prepaid, and failure rates climb from 22% to 35% as delivery slips from 2 days to 5+ ([Shipway, India carrier data, 2024](https://mediabrief.com/shipnotes-reveals-26-rto-rate-on-cod-orders-across-india/) — the best published COD dataset; MENA carriers publish nothing).

So week 2 installs the confirmation layer from the [COD WhatsApp Script Bank](../swipe-files/whatsapp/cod-whatsapp-script-bank.md): confirmation within the hour, delivery-day heads-up, same-day failed-attempt recovery. WhatsApp automated commerce flows convert at 8-15%+ with 70-90% open rates ([Chatarmin KPI study](https://chatarmin.com/en/customers/whatsapp-kpi-study-ecommerce-benchmarks)) — in COD, this isn't marketing, it's collections.

### Step 5 — Measure what actually matters (Week 3+)

The new weekly scorecard, in order:

```
1. Delivered revenue (not placed revenue)
2. Delivery rate            ← the profit lever
3. Confirmation rate
4. Checkout completion      ← the fixed leak, watched for regression
5. ROAS on DELIVERED orders ← the only honest ROAS in COD
6. Then, and only then: CTR, CPM, creative metrics
```

And the ads? Scaled back up only after checkout completion recovered — because scaling ads into a leaking funnel buys leaks in bulk.

## 🧠 The transferable lessons

- **Diagnose in funnel order, not in panic order.** The loudest metric (ROAS) is downstream of everything; the broken layer is usually quieter. The [Funnel Decomposition Skill](../skills/funnel-decomposition/SKILL.md) makes this automatic.
- **"What changed recently?" beats most analysis.** Sudden collapses have specific causes; gradual declines have structural ones. Ask the question before opening a single dashboard.
- **In COD, the funnel ends at the doorstep.** Any diagnosis that stops at "purchase" is auditing a fiction. Delivery rate belongs next to ROAS on every report.
- **Never scale spend into a broken stage.** Fix conversion leaks first — ads multiply whatever funnel they're pointed at, including the holes.
- **Run this on your own store:** Creative Diagnosis → checkout-stage benchmarks ([CRO benchmarks](../benchmarks/cro-ecommerce-benchmarks.md)) → COD Profit Funnel rates → WhatsApp layer. Same order, your numbers.
