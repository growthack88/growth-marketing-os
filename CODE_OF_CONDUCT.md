# Code of Conduct

This is a practitioner community. The rules are short:

1. **Critique work, not people.** "This benchmark is outdated, here's a newer source" is welcome. Personal attacks are not.
2. **No harassment of any kind** — including on the basis of language, nationality, gender, religion, or experience level. Both English and Arabic speakers are first-class citizens here.
3. **No spam or self-promotion** in issues, discussions, or PRs. Contributing a genuinely useful asset with your credit on it is the welcome form of self-promotion.
4. **Honesty over polish.** Invented numbers, fake results, and unlabeled AI filler are treated as bad-faith contributions.

Violations: first a warning, then a block. Report issues to hi@mahmoudomar.com or via a private GitHub report.
