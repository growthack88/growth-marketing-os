# Social Media Benchmarks (2026, Sourced)

> Organic social benchmarks with methodology attached: engagement rates by platform from the two major studies (which disagree, for knowable reasons), LinkedIn format multipliers, short-form video completion, post half-life, and the MENA platform landscape.

## ⚡ What it does

Social benchmarks are where denominators go to die: engagement "per follower" vs "per impression" differ 10x, averages vs medians differ 3x, and most posts quoting benchmarks never say which. This file presents the current numbers from the named large-sample studies with their bases labeled — so you compare your accounts against the right math, and against the right country when the audience is MENA.

## 🎯 When I use it (real scenario)

Two jobs: sanity-checking content performance (is 0.4% Instagram engagement failure or the industry median? — it's roughly the median) and platform strategy for MENA audiences, where the global picture misleads badly — Snapchat is a top-3 platform in KSA, X penetration in Saudi runs ~4x global norms, and Facebook is still Egypt's largest platform. The country tables below are the ones I check before any "which platform should we be on?" conversation.

## 📋 The Benchmarks

### 1 · Engagement rate by platform (two studies, two methodologies — both shown)

**Rival IQ 2025** (median ER per post, **by followers**, ~2,100 brands): TikTok **1.73%** · Instagram **0.36%** (−16% YoY) · Facebook **0.063%** (−36%) · X **0.029%** (−48%). [Source](https://www.rivaliq.com/blog/social-media-industry-benchmark-report/)

**Socialinsider 2026 edition** (avg ER, ~70M posts, 2025 data): TikTok **3.70%** (+49% YoY) · Instagram **0.48%** · Facebook **0.15%** · X **0.12%**. LinkedIn: **5.2% avg ER by impressions** — a different base; never compare it to the follower-based numbers. [Source](https://www.socialinsider.io/social-media-benchmarks)

Directional agreement: TikTok highest and rising, X lowest and collapsing fastest. Industry spread (Rival IQ): Higher Education engages ~4x the median; Health & Beauty lowest. Engagement is shifting passive — TikTok shares/post +45% while comments −24% (Socialinsider 2025).

### 2 · LinkedIn (the deepest-studied platform right now)

**Van der Blom Algorithm InSights 2025** (1.8M posts, 58K profiles, 31K pages). [Source](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/richardvanderblom_chapter-1-algorithm-insights-report-2025-activity-7322514599126130688-Q895) · [summary](https://authoredup.com/blog/linkedin-algorithm)

| Finding | Number |
|---|---|
| Organic views / engagement / follower growth YoY | −50% / −25% / −59% (98% of users lost reach) |
| Format reach multipliers (profiles) | polls 1.64x · documents/carousels 1.45x · images 1.18x · video 1.10x · text 0.88x |
| Optimal cadence | 3-5 posts/week; >1/day suppresses reach |
| Golden hour | first 30-60 min of engagement decides distribution |
| Documents | 8-10 slides optimal; low completion now penalized |
| Saves | worth ~5x the reach value of likes |

**Metricool 2026** (674K posts): personal profiles **2.60%** ER vs company pages **1.74%** — the person outperforms the logo. [Source](https://metricool.com/linkedin-statistics/) Format ER by impressions (Socialinsider): native documents 7.0% > multi-image 6.45% > video 6.0% > single image 5.3% > text 4.5% > external links 3.25%.

### 3 · Short-form video

| Metric | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Completion rates | TikTok ~65% · YouTube Shorts ~70% · Reels ~50% | [Shortimize, 2025](https://www.shortimize.com/blog/compare-watch-time-tiktok-vs-reels-vs-shorts) |
| TikTok length shift | >60s videos now average 16.2K views vs 5.4K for <30s — longer wins | [Socialinsider video study, 16M posts, 2025](https://www.socialinsider.io/social-media-benchmarks/social-media-video-statistics) |
| Reels/FB Reels sweet spot | 60-90 seconds | same |
| First-3-seconds retention | **no measured public benchmark exists** — the "hook in 3 seconds" rule is practitioner consensus; closest measured: viewers decide in ~8s | Retention Rabbit via [Stripo Research, 2025](https://research.stripo.email/youtube-benchmarks) |

### 4 · YouTube

CTR: half of all videos land between **2-10%** (YouTube's own published range); by source: subscriber feed 7-10%, search 4-6%, suggested 2-4%. Retention: platform average **23.7%**; only 16.8% of videos exceed 50%; **55% of viewers leave within the first 60 seconds**; how-to/educational retains best (42.1%) vs vlogs (21.5%). ([Stripo Research/Retention Rabbit, 2025](https://research.stripo.email/youtube-benchmarks) — small study; YouTube publishes no official retention benchmark.) That first-60-seconds cliff is why the [YouTube Script Outline](../prompts/content/youtube-script-outline.md) bans slow intros.

### 5 · Post half-life (how long content works for you)

TikTok ~0 min (front-loaded burst) · Instagram ~20h · LinkedIn ~24h · YouTube ~9 days · Pinterest ~3.8 months. ([Graffius half-life study, 5.6M posts, 2024-25](https://www.scottgraffius.com/blog/files/lifespan-halflife-of-social-media-posts-update-for-2025.html) — single-author, order-of-magnitude guide.) Strategy implication: feed platforms are distribution, search platforms (YouTube/Pinterest) are assets — plan the [repurposing matrix](../prompts/content/content-repurposing-matrix.md) accordingly.

### 6 · MENA platform landscape (DataReportal, 2025-26)

**Egypt** ([Digital 2026](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-egypt), Oct 2025 data): 98.2M internet users (82.7%) · Facebook **51.6M — still the #1 platform** · YouTube 49.3M · TikTok 48.8M · Instagram 21.7M (+13.9%) · Snapchat 20.6M · LinkedIn 15M · X 4.6M (−15.4%).

**Saudi Arabia** ([Digital 2025](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2025-saudi-arabia)): 33.9M internet users (99%) · TikTok 34.1M · YouTube 27.2M · **Snapchat 24.7M (72% — globally exceptional)** · Instagram 16.9M · Facebook 16.4M · **X 15.7M (46% — ~4x global norms)** · LinkedIn 11M.

**UAE** ([Digital 2025](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2025-united-arab-emirates)): 11.1M internet users (99%) · TikTok 11.3M · Facebook 9.7M · **LinkedIn 9.4M (84% — among the world's highest)** · YouTube 8.25M · Instagram 7.6M.

Global average daily social time: 2h21m ([DataReportal Digital 2026 Global](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2026-global-overview-report)). ⚠️ Ad-reach figures exceeding 100% of adults (KSA TikTok) reflect duplicate/non-resident accounts — ad reach ≠ real users.

The MENA read: platform strategy is country strategy. An Egypt plan without Facebook, a KSA plan without Snapchat/X, or a UAE B2B plan without LinkedIn each ignores that market's actual center of gravity.

## 🧠 How to use benchmarks

- **Name the denominator before comparing** — per-follower vs per-impression differ by an order of magnitude; medians (Rival IQ) describe a typical post, averages (Socialinsider) are inflated by viral outliers.
- **Benchmark trends, not levels**: everyone's Facebook engagement fell ~36% — yours falling 20% is outperformance, not failure.
- **Formats are the free lunch**: on LinkedIn, the same idea as a document/carousel earns ~1.45x the reach of the same idea as text. Format selection is the cheapest reach multiplier available.
- **MENA audiences: check the country table first** — global platform benchmarks assume a platform mix that doesn't exist in Cairo or Riyadh.
